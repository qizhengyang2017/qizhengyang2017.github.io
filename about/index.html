about

我叫戚正阳，Jeremy17 是我在网上常用的昵称，因为华裔NBA球员林书豪的英文名叫Jeremy，17是因为我出生于1994年2月17日。林书豪在纽约打球的时候，球衣印的是17号，他说17有上帝与我同在的意思 。我不是基督徒，但有时会看一下圣经。因为我姨婆是基督徒，并且曾经会时不时给我打电话。我修过英语的双学位，因为那段时间很爱看文学小说。但是我没有拿到学位。我觉得当时我应该选择计算机专业。

My name is Zhengyang Qi, Jeremy17 is my nickname on the Internet. Jeremy is the name of a basketball player called Jeremy Lin. I was born on 17 February 1994. When Lin played in NewYork City, his number was 17, which means “God is with me”. I am not Christian, but I will read Bible.

我正在攻读硕士学位，现在在华中农业大学园艺植物生物学教育部重点实验室。2019年夏天毕业之后，我可能去另一个团队攻读博士学位，如果上帝眷顾我的话。我现在的导师是 Prof. Wenwu Guo。

I’m getting my master’s degree in key laboratory of horticulture biology (MoE), Huazhong Agricultural University. And I may continue my study for a doctoral’s degree in another team after graduating on the summer of 2019. My supervisor is Prof. Wenwu Guo.

我刚经历了学校的120周年校庆，这是一个神圣而庄严的时刻。我很高兴当了校庆志愿者。我还有一张校庆照片获了奖，我很喜欢给我发的奖品（下图），是一个带有蝴蝶翅膀的钥匙扣，颜色很好看，有部分颜色像火焰鸟羽毛的颜色。等到2019年夏天，我会在这里待够七年，毫无疑问这里是我的第二故乡。

I just got through the 120th anniversary of HZAU. It was a signature moment. I was happy to be a volunteer. I received a key ring as an award. I like the color of it. I will be here for 7 years in 2019. No doubt, here is my home away from home.

我在研一的时候开始接触编程，我很尊敬程序员这个群体，他们很多人都有改变世界的梦想。我很赞赏开放源代码的思想，并且尊敬那些为开源社区做出杰出贡献的黑客们。我推崇Unix哲学，喜欢在Linux系统下去完成一些工作。不过我并不喜欢把这种爱好变为一种类似于“宗教信仰”的东西，我通常会用最方便的工具去完成我要做的事情，不论它是 Linux 还是 Windows 、R 、 Python 或是 Excel ……

I knew little things about programming before my first year getting my master’s degree. I appreciate the group of Programmers because many of them have a dream of changing the world. I also appreciate the idea of Open Source and respect those hackers who made outstanding contribution to the open source community. I like the philosophy of Unix and I also like to finish my jobs in Linux. But I don’t like to treat this enthusiasm as somthing related to religious. I’ll use the most convenient tools to get things done, no matter whether it is Linux or Windows, R or Pyhton or even Excel …

作为一名大农学专业的学生，我感兴趣的方向是如何用生物信息的工具解决生物学的问题，并将其用于农业生产，我觉得这是我学了那么多年农业之后的一种责任。另外，我也关注一些人工智能方面的东西，我渴望了解人工智能将会如何促进农业现代化。

As a student of Agriculture, my research interests are solving bioloy problems by using informatics tools and then applying them to the reality. Besides, I’m also interested in how the methodologies and ideas of Artificial Intelligence can be borrowed to the process of modernization of agriculture.

同我身边的许多人一样，我很喜欢看电影。我也喜欢书法作品，喜欢那种一气呵成的感觉，我一直梦想着能够有时间去练习书法。爬山是我喜欢的运动，我在家乡的时候，节假日经常会去爬山。家乡的那座山叫“尖峰山”，是金华人寄托乡愁的一座山，坊间流传一句话，说是金华人见不到尖峰山就会哭。在学校，我经常打篮球，暑假会去游泳。

Just like many people around me, I like watching films. And I also like calligraphic works, because I like the feeling of no interruption. when I have free time, I want to learn Chinese calligraphy. I like mountain climbing very much. There is a mountain called “JianFeng Mountain” which has a special meaning to people in my hometown. In school, I oftern play basketball and go to swimming in summer holiday.
